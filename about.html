About Me - Behind Creaxaura

Hello! I am anime artist, programmer, and content creator. My dream is to one day be a part of the games and animation industries and eventually have my own studio.

My art specialty is digital anime and cartoon characters, as they have inspired me ever since I was a child. I love more cartoony styles with a more serious storyline.

My favorite genre for anything from comics, tv shows, and video games is mystery. Most of my games and stories will have a mystery element attached that you'll have to solve.

Story driven games will be the main focus of my work, however there will be some exceptions to the rule.

I'm currently making smaller mini-games, stickers, and practicing my art skills. I also create content via my instagram, youtube, and twitch channel.
