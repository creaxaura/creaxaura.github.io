What is the Light Pollen Project?

The Light Pollen Project is a fantasy crime drama centered on the mysterious supernatural events of an area known as Quaios County. Many of these events have been in been very small and unnoticeable, but ever since the late 1990s, these events have been even more prevalent than they've been in a long time. With these events getting more visible, the mayors of the area have tried to best to suppress these events, but the more they try to suppress them, the bigger the next events become... Will they be able to stop this cycle before it becomes too late?
